Mobile app UI design for BrainFit - Dashboard/Home Screen

Visual Style:

- Primary accent: #E94560 (coral red) for CTAs and alerts

- Typography: SF Pro Display / Inter, with SF Mono for data numbers
- Design aesthetic: Neuro-Tech × Nike Training Club × Oura Ring

  - 呼吸工具箱 (teal icon)

Visual Effects:
- Neumorphic card design with subtle shadows

- Minimalist linear icons (2px stroke, rounded caps)
- No illustrations, no cartoon characters
- High contrast for readability
- Generous whitespace between sections

Mood: Professional, scientific, motivating, premium fitness app aesthetic
Technical: High-fidelity mobile UI mockup, realistic iOS design patterns
